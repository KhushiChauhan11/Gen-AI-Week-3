Insurance policy guide:
• Coverage: hospitalization, outpatient (OPD) limits, and defined sum insured.
• Premium: employee's share may be deducted monthly; employer may co-pay.
• Claims: cashless at network hospitals; reimbursement otherwise.
• Exclusions: cosmetic procedures, pre-existing waiting periods.
• Claim workflow: inform TPA, provide ID, submit bills and discharge summary.
